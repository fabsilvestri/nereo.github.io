About the Project

NEREO is a PRIN2022 research project focused on developing innovative approaches to Neural Information Retrieval and Natural Language Processing systems. Our research explores the integration of upstream IR systems with cascading NLP pipelines to create more efficient and effective information access solutions.

Research Objectives

🔍

Upstream IR Systems

Developing and improving neural retrieval systems including PLAID, DIME, CoDIME, and ECLIPSE.

🔗

Cascading IR/NLP

Exploring the integration of IR and NLP systems through innovative cascading approaches.

⚡

Power of Noise

Investigating noise injection techniques for improving neural information retrieval performance.

🛠️

Open Source Tools

Creating openly available prototypes and tools for the research community.
